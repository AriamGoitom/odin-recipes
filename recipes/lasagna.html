Lasagna

Description

Here is a picture of a home made lasagna.

The lasagna was made with love and passion.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Meat
  • Seasoning
  • Tomato sause
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Chop onions and garlic and fry in a pan.
  2. Add the meat and the seasoning in to the pan.
  3. Add the tomato sause.
  4. Mix everything in a oven dish.
  5. Put some cheese on top.
  6. Put the oven dish in the oven for about 45 minutes in 200 celcius degree.
  7. Voila, enjoy your food!
