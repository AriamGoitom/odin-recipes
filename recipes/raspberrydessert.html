Raspberry Dessert

Description

Here is a picture of a raspberry dessert.

The dessert is made with just three simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • frozen raspberries
  • greek yogurt
  • brown sugar

Steps

  1. Put some frozen raspberries in glasses.
  2. Add greek yogurt in the glass and sprinkle a thick layer of brown sugar over the glass.
  3. Refrigerate uncovered for 24 hours so the sugar crystallizes.
  4. Voila, there you go!
